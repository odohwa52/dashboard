Investing Tips
Core principles for KR retail

ISA Account

Use a Korean ISA wrapper — the first ₩2M of gains are tax-free, and only the excess gets taxed at 9.9% (vs 15.4% otherwise).

DCA Mechanics

Set a fixed amount on a fixed date each month. Removes timing emotion. Toss Bank can automate it.

USD/KRW Risk

If KRW strengthens 10%, your USD-denominated S&P returns drop 10% in KRW. Consider hedged ETFs if FX exposure worries you.

TER Comparison

360750 (0.07%) vs 360200 (0.05%) — the spread compounds. Always check expense ratios before picking your wrapper.

Rebalancing

Annual is enough. Quarterly leaks fees. If allocation drifts >5pp from target, that's the trigger.

Don't Time

Time in market beats timing the market. You will be wrong about tops and bottoms more than half the time.
